Brand Promise

Body & Soul creates an immersive brunch experience where soulful music, comfort food, and craft beverages come together to energize both body and spirit.

Brand Story

Body & Soul was born from a passion for bringing people together through two universal languages: music and food. In a world constantly rushing, we create a space where time slows down, allowing guests to truly connect with themselves, the music, and their community.

Starting in local breweries, our popup events transform ordinary spaces into vibrant gatherings where soul music fills the air, comfort food satisfies cravings, and handcrafted beverages refresh the spirit. Body & Soul isn't just a brunch—it's a multisensory experience that leaves you feeling both nourished and inspired.

Experience Design

Atmosphere

  • DJ booth visible and central to the space
  • Casual seating with some communal tables to encourage interaction
  • Dance space that naturally forms as the event progresses
  • Warm lighting with subtle gold accents
  • Minimalist decor that allows the brewery's character to shine through

Signature Elements

  • Custom playlist QR codes on tables
  • "Soul Food" menu with music-inspired dish names
  • Specialty non-alcoholic "Rhythm Refreshers" that complement the brewery's beer offerings
  • Featured local artist/DJ spotlight each event
  • Community "Soul Board" where guests can leave notes about their experience
